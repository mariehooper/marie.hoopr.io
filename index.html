About

I'm passionate about building products that people love to use.

I graduated with a degree in Finance only to realize I was more interested in helping people work more efficiently using technology, so I learned everything I could about frontend development using online resources and by building a lot of side (read: crappy) projects.

Since then, I've been a project manager, designer, lead engineer, and a JavaScript instructor. I have led teams of designers and developers and love helping others discover how exciting it is to use technology to design solutions for such a wide breadth of problems. I have experience building production React and Vue.js apps using both REST and GraphQL APIs, utilizing CI/CD pipelines, writing automated tests, and using just about every styling approach you can think of.

In my spare time, I've led a JavaScript meetup, mentored junior developers, and given several presentations on a range of frontend related technologies to local groups.
